1. Coefficient and Formula of Linear Expansion

The coefficient of linear thermal expansion (CTE, a, or a1) is a material property that is indicative of the extent to which a material expands upon heating. Different substances expand by different amounts. Over small temperature ranges, the thermal expansion of uniform linear objects is proportional to temperature change.

Thermal expansion can generate detrimental internal stress when a structural part is heated and kept at constant length.
